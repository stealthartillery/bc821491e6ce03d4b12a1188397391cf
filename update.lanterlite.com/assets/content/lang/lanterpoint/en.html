بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيم

Lanter Point (LPoint) is the accumulation of scores obtained for user achievements when using the Lanterlite application. LPoint can be exchanged for LSilver to buy digital goods in LStore which can be used on all Lanterlite applications.

To get LPoint, users must sign-in to Lanterlite app, then take the challenge or test available on the Lanterlite app. Users can exchange LPoint with LSilver by buying LSilver using LPoint on the official Lanter Store website, store.lanterlite.com
