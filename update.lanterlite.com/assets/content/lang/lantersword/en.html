1. Description

Lanter Sword is an MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game) Game created by the Lanterlite Team with the aim of making it easier to study Islam for people who like to play online MMORPG games..

In Lanter Sword, the characters used are endeavored to obey Islamic law. As an example:
1. There is no monster object,
2. Physical character of the player who intentionally made disabled (do not have faces and fingers),
3. Background music in the form of natural sounds instead of music.

Meanwhile, Lanter Sword implements several features that are commonly possessed by an MMORPG. As an example:
1. The quest system that is related to Islam and not,
2. Trading System,
3. Friend System,
4. Duel System,
4. Guild Battle System.

In Lanter Sword there are a number of premium items that can be purchased using only Pearl. Pearl can be purchased online using Lanter Silver via Lanter Store.

2. How to Play

Players during playing on Lanter Sword can do activities: questing, chatting, trading, dueling, battle, etc.

2.1. Questing

The questing activity is completing various kinds of quests available in each NPC in the Lanter Sword. Each complete the quest, players will get gold, experience (exp), and sometimes get items. By increasing experience, players can reach higher levels so they can do everything that has minimum level requirements. For example: using special items, creating guilds, etc.

2.2. Chatting

During play, players can also chat with other players with shortcuts, as follows:

Shortcut Function Example
! Shout in current map !Hello
@ Whisp other player @Enigma Hello
^ Shout in all map ^Hello

2.3. Trading

Trading is the activity of buying and selling items on Lanter Sword. Step:
1. Poke Player,
2. Choose Trade,
3. Wait for confirmation,
4. After the trade screen is displayed, select the item you want to give,
5. Press the Cross button to select / cancel item,
6. Press the L2 key to move the cursor to another screen,
7. Press the Triangle button to close the screen trade,

2.3.1. Insert Gold

1. Move the cursor to the top left screen,
2. Press the Circle button, insert the nominal, then select ok.

2.3.2. Insert Pearl

1. Move the cursor to the top left screen,
2. Press the Square button, insert the nominal, then select ok.

2.3.3. Agreement

1. Move the cursor to the top left screen,
2. Press the L1 button once, then the items and gold and pearl will not be changed by both parties.
3. Press the L1 button for the second time, the item and gold and pearl will move to the opposite transaction.

2.4. Dueling

Duel is a combat activity using weapons carried out by two players. The time given during the duel is 60 seconds. The player who can defeat his opponent before 60 seconds is the winner. Step:
1. Poke Player,
2. Choose Duel,
3. Wait for confirmation,
4. After the timer shows the number 0, then the duel can begin.

2.5. Battle

Battle is a battle activity using weapons carried out by two guilds. Members who do not join a guild cannot use this feature. The time given during the battle is 5 minutes. A guild that can defeat all of its opponent's guild members before 5 minutes, the guild is the winner. Step:
1. Poke Player (Guild Leader),
2. Choose Battle,
3. Wait for confirmation,
4. After the timer shows the number 0, then the battle can begin.

2.6. Guild

A guild is a group of players with the same vision and mission. To make a guild, players must reach at least level 3 and require 2000 gold. There are several powers that can be exercised by the leader of the guild (such as recruiting other players, battles, removing members, etc.). Steps to make a guild:
1. Poke NPC Manar in Archendia Town Square,
2. Choose Create Guild,
3. Insert guild name,
4. Choose ok.

3. Insert alphanumeric on input

There are several things that require alphanumeric input during play. For example: answering quests, writing guild names, entering nominal gold or pearls on screen trades, etc. Enter alphanumeric in the input column can be done by:
1. Move the cursor to the input position (use the Top, Bottom, Left, Right button),
2. Then press the Start button, after the keyboard appears then start typing alphanumeric,
3. When finished, press the Start button.
