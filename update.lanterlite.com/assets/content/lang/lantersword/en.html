Lanter Sword
Lanterlite MMORPG

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيم

Lanter Sword is an MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game) game created by Lanterlite Team with aim to make it easy for people who love playing games to learn about Islam through games.

In Lanter Sword, there are various quests related to Islam. There is also a trading system, friendship, duel, battle, which players can try during play.

At Lanter Sword there are a number of premium items that can be purchased only using Pearl. Pearl can be purchased online using Lanter Silver through the Lanter Store.
