Islamic Search Engine

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيم

Islam is the religion of all people. Every human being has the right to learn it. However, the limitations of facilities and capabilities make it difficult for humans to obtain knowledge about true Islam/ from reliable references. This is a chance for Lanterlite team to help, by making an Islamic search engine application called Lanter Search.

Lanter Search is an Islamic content-based search engine sourced from several reference books that have been recognized in Islam such as the Qur'an, Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, and so on. Lanter Search can explore a topic in Islam and then provide information related to it in the form of propositions in Arabic and translations, hadith numbers, narrators of the hadith, and the degree of the hadith.

Lanter Search can be accessed from web search.lanterlite.com. Also can be installed on Mobile with Android OS here or Desktop with Windows OS here. This app is free for the main features, while additional features can be obtained by purchasing a license at Lanter Store.
