Being a memorizer of the Qur'an for a Muslim is an achievement that has an impact on increasing self degree and quality in the sight of Allah. Various methods of memorizing the Qur'an are made to facilitate memorizers of the Qur'an. These methods are oriented towards audio, visual, or both. The variety of ways to memorize the Qur'an in the midst of the community makes the Lanterlite team participate too by making an application to memorize the Qur'an using the bookshelf method, which also focused on understanding the meaning as well as the ease of reading it. It's called the Lanter Quran.

Lanter Quran is a Digital Quran application that has the feature of memorization, translation by words or by sentences, and transliteration (pronunciation) in each verse. The memorization feature of the Lanter Quran applies the bookshelf method described in this article. Also the Quran Lanter is equipped with a verse-copy feature that users can use to share the verse with friends.

Lanter Quran can be installed on Mobile with Android OS here. Also can be installed on Desktop with Windows OS here. This app is free for the main features, while additional features can be obtained by purchasing a license at Lanter Store.
