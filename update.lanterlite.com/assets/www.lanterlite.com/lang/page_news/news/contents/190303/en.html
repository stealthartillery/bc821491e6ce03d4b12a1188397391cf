Lanterlite Dual Languages
Lanterlite now supports for Bahasa and English language.
Mar 3, 2019

Bismillah.

Lanterlite site now fully supports Bahasa and English language. So that for those of You who are from outside of Indonesia will not missed the latest information from Lanterlite. Also, currently Lanterlite site is able to automatically detect languages that You might use everyday based on the location of Your devices. In the future, Lanterlite will try to load Arabic as the third language published on Lanterlite site, Insyaa Allah.

In addition, Alhamdulillah, Lanterlite site has a new article page which will be intended to publish articles related to Islam and technology that are comprehensive in compliance with Islamic law, Insyaa Allah. Also, from the server security side, it has been improved at several points so that it is better than before in order to support the security and convenience of our users.
