Available on Windows Store
Enlite and Qulite support on Desktop.
Apr 12, 2019

Bismillah.

Enlite and Qulite apps now support desktop platforms for the Windows operating system and are available in the Windows Store. This is the beginning of Lanterlite apps to be able to start running on Laptop and PC devices as Desktop apps. Insyaa Allah, as soon as possible, Lanterlite apps will begin to expand to other operating systems such as MacOS and Linux.

Feature developments have also been made on Enlite and Qulite, one of which is the account information and management feature. Insyaa Allah, Enlite and Qulite will continue to be developed in order to facilitate our users in deepening Islam from any aspect.
