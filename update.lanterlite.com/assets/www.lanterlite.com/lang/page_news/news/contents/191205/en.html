بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيم

Lanter Square is a social networking application designed by the Lanterlite team to make it easier for people to interact with one another by minimizing bad posts such as the dissemination of information that is not suitable for public reading. This is because the Lanterlite team will continue to monitor the events in Lanter Square while maintaining the privacy of users during interactions. Insyaa Allah.

Messages sent personally will be encrypted in such a way as to maintain user privacy during interaction. In addition there are group feature to make it easier for users to collaborate with other users. The Lanterlite Team will also use Lanter Square as a means of interacting both between team members and other users.

The Lanterlite Team will also create a Lanter Sword group (ID Lanter Square: @lantersword) to make it easier for the MMORPG Lanter Sword players to interact between players as well as with the GM. Insyaa Allah.

Thankyou.
