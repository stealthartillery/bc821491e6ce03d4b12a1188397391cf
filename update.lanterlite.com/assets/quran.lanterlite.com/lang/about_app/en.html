Bookshelves Method
An approach to memorize the Quran
Ifan Dhani
Mar 1, 2019

Bismillah.

Being an Hafidzh of Quran is the dream of every believer. Muslims are promised the highest degree of Heaven for those who memorize the Qur'an. Therefore, several methods of memorizing the Qur'an try to be done, such as memorizing methods using colors, memorizing by optimizing the senses of sight, hearing, movement, and so on.

In this article, I will share how to memorize Quran using library method. I have never heard of a method similar to this before, but this is only the result of the form of memorizing the Qur'an that I want.

This method uses the concept of a library consisting of a number of bookshelves arranged in a row. In this method, space (space for storing books) from the shelf is arranged into 10 rows and 5 columns (10x5).

Pic. Bookshelves Method for Quran Memorizing.

Each space stores the first word from one verse, for one surah in the Quran. Also each space has a number that is equal to the verse number. So we just imagine this bookshelf when memorizing a surah in the Qur'an.

One of the benefits that I can get by this way is that I can slowly see patterns from a number of verses in one surah, as well as patterns from a number of surahs. One of the patterns I meant is the pattern of the relationship between the verse number (or surah) and the meaning of the verse. For example, surah Ar-Rahman (no. 55) and Al-Waqi'ah (no. 56) together discuss the people of hell starting with verse 41, where the previous verses on each surah discuss heaven or about the greatness of God. Masyaa Allah. So that we can deepen our knowledge regarding the miracle of the arrangement of surahs and verses in the Quran.

This memorization method is already in the Lanter Quran Android app that you can get in Play Store.
