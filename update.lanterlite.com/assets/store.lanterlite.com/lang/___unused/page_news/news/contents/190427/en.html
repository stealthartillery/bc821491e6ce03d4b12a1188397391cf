Lanterlite Trade Center
Lanterlite Trade Center has released.
Apr 12, 2019

Bismillah.

Today, Lanterlite released an Islamic marketplace application called Lanterlite Trading Center (LTC). LTC is a marketplace that applies Islamic principles in trading, such as no fraud, harming some party, selling products and using pictures that are not permitted, and other things that are prohibited in Islam. LTC was made to support one of Lanterlite's missions in spreading Islamic Sharia to the public.
